Rangituhi Walk

A private bus will drop students off at the start of the Rangituhi walkway. Teachers will help students navigate the path to the lookout and then up to the summit of Rangituhi. From here, students will traverse the tops and then pop down into Te Ngahere o Tawa. Students will spend more time on the walk learning about reading maps, trees and leaves, local places of interest from viewpoints, etc. There is also a $50 prezzy card as a prize for activities students can complete on the way.Forms
